Privacy

In terms of privacy, I don't collect any personal information about this website's visitors.

To better understand users and improve accessibility and design, I use Statcounter to see details like how much traffic this website receives, what devices are used to access it, and the numbers of visitors from different locations. This service provides aggregate data, and I don't collect any personal information about this website's visitors from those services.

I encourage you to view Statcounter's privacy policy if you're concerned about how this third party service works on this website, what data is collected and what it is used for.

If you contact me, I will not share your email with any other individuals, businesses or organizations without your permission.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, or any other issues that are important to you, please contact me.
