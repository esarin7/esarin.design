Hi, Nice to Meet You!

I'm a short and curious
content creator + web developer.

I'm an experienced content creator, communications strategist and social media specialist. For the past decade I've worked in the international development sector - a very different world from web development.

Whether reporting with journalists in Malawi, re-writing the digital content of an international think-tank, or training Colombian farmers on the use of digital tools, I've constantly found myself working on website revamps, upgrades or implementations.

During my ten years of experience I've seen all sorts of wonderful, useful, powerful information and eye-opening stories whither in office bookshelves or drawers as they're trapped inside reports and pamphlets that no one feels curious to read.

I love useful information and stories well-told. My heart aches when I see all the valuable knowledge that goes unseen.

I see code as the perfect tool amplify these stories and information and present them in exciting and engaging ways.
