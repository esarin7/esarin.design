Nice to Meet You

I'm a short and curious content creator + web developer.

For the past ten years I've done content development, communications strategy, and social media in the international development sector - a very different world from web development.

Whether training journalists in Malawi, re-writing the digital content of an international think-tank, or training Colombian farmers on the use of digital tools, I've frequently ended up working on website revamps, upgrades or implementations.

During my ten years of experience I've seen all sorts of wonderful, useful, powerful information and eye-opening stories whither in office bookshelves or drawers as they're trapped inside reports and pamphlets that no one feels compelled to read.

I love useful information and stories well-told. My heart aches when I see all the valuable knowledge that goes unseen.

I see code as the perfect tool amplify these stories and information and present them in exciting ways.
